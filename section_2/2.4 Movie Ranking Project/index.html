The Best Movies According to Sumit K

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Pursuit of Happyness

One of my favourite movies which I can watch anytime and won't be bored. This movie beautifully captures the struggles in life and how one should be persistent to make his situation better. The acting feels very natural and all in all it is a very good package.

3 Idiots

A must watch for students I would say. The movie is filled with laughs and lessons. For all parents who are running after the grades of there children should watch it at least once.

Ready Player One

Viewers who like gaming should watch it to experience what can be the future of VR gaming. As per personal recommendations I would have liked if there were easter eggs of real life famous games.
